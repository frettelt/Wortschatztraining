Tips for your audio guide

Short sentences! The listener cannot read — only listen. Keep it simple.
Talk to the listener. Use "you" and questions like "Have you ever …?"
Not too fast! Practise reading aloud before you record.
Check your pronunciation in a dictionary or online if you are not sure.
Each person in your group should say at least one sentence.
Start and end your audio guide — welcome the listener and say goodbye!
